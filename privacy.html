🛢️ FILRUP Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

FILRUP LLC ("we", "our", or "us") operates the FILRUP mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Location data: We access your device's GPS location to automatically detect the nearest gas station name and address when you log a fuel purchase. Location is only accessed while you are actively using the app.
  • Voice data: When you use the voice input feature, your spoken words are processed by Apple's Speech Recognition service to convert your fuel purchase statement into text. We do not store your voice recordings.
  • Fuel log data: Fuel purchase entries you create (amount, fuel type, station name, date, gallons, payment method) are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage.
  • Vehicle information: Vehicle names you add are stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Location is used solely to identify nearby gas stations via the Google Places API.
  • Voice input is processed to extract fuel purchase details (amount, fuel type, gallons).
  • Fuel log data is used to display your spending history, statistics, and reports within the app.
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

FILRUP uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Places API: Used to identify nearby gas stations. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Speech Recognition: Used to process voice input on iOS. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic Claude API: Used to parse fuel purchase details from your spoken statements. Your transcript is sent to Anthropic's API for processing. Subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare Workers: Our backend is hosted on Cloudflare Workers. Requests are processed securely. Subject to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage

  • All fuel log entries and vehicle data are stored locally on your device only.
  • We do not operate a database or cloud storage for your personal fuel records.
  • Deleting the app will permanently remove all locally stored data.

5. Permissions We Request

  • Location (While Using App): To detect nearby gas stations automatically.
  • Microphone: To capture your voice when logging fuel purchases by speaking.
  • Speech Recognition: To convert your spoken fuel purchase into text for processing.
  • Photo Library: May be used in future versions to attach receipt photos to fuel entries.

6. Children's Privacy

FILRUP is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. API keys are stored securely as encrypted secrets on our Cloudflare backend and are never exposed to the client app.

8. Your Rights

  • You can delete all your data at any time by deleting the app.
  • You can revoke location, microphone, and speech recognition permissions at any time through your device settings.
  • You can contact us to request information about data we may hold.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
