About FILRUP

FILRUP is a voice-powered fuel tracker. Just tap the microphone and say what you spent — for example, "15 gallons, regular, 42 dollars" — and FILRUP logs it for you in seconds. Track multiple vehicles, view your monthly spending, and use the app in English or Spanish.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log a fuel purchase?

Tap the microphone button on the home screen and say your purchase out loud, such as "15 gallons, regular, 42 dollars." You can also enter purchases manually if you prefer.

Why does FILRUP ask for microphone access?

The microphone is used only to capture your voice when you choose to log a fuel purchase by speaking. You're always in control of when it's used.

Why does FILRUP ask for my location?

Location is used to automatically detect the gas station you're at and fill in its name and address when you log a purchase, so you don't have to type it. You can also enter the station manually.

Does FILRUP work in Spanish?

Yes! FILRUP works in both English and Spanish. You can choose your language in the app's settings.

Can I track more than one vehicle?

Absolutely. You can add multiple vehicles and track fuel spending separately for each one.

How do I delete my data?

Your fuel data is stored on your device. You can remove entries within the app, and uninstalling the app removes your stored data. For any questions, email us at hello@filrup.app.
