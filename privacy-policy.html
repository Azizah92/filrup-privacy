⛽ FILRUP Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 28, 2026

FILRUP ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the FILRUP mobile application.

Information We Collect

FILRUP collects the following information to provide its services:

  • Location data: We access your device's GPS location to detect nearby gas stations. Location data is used only within the app and is not stored on our servers or shared with third parties.
  • Voice/microphone data: When you use the voice entry feature, your voice is recorded briefly to detect fuel expense details. Voice recordings are processed locally and are not stored or transmitted to our servers.
  • Fuel expense data: Fuel logs, amounts, vehicle information, and related data you enter are stored locally on your device using secure local storage.

How We Use Your Information

  • To detect nearby gas stations using your GPS location
  • To process voice input and extract fuel expense details
  • To display your fuel expense history and spending statistics
  • To improve app functionality and user experience

Third Party Services

FILRUP uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Places API: Used to identify nearby gas stations based on your location. Google's privacy policy applies: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Anthropic API: Used to process voice transcripts and extract fuel expense details. Anthropic's privacy policy applies: anthropic.com/privacy

Data Storage

All fuel expense data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal fuel data. Your data stays on your phone.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. We do not share your location data, voice recordings, or fuel expense data with any third parties beyond what is necessary to operate the app (Google Places for station detection, Anthropic for voice processing).

In-App Purchases

FILRUP offers a subscription through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not collect or store any payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies to all transactions.

Children's Privacy

FILRUP is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data stored on your device through the app
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Revoke location or microphone permissions at any time through your device settings

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. All data is stored locally on your device using secure storage provided by the iOS operating system.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
