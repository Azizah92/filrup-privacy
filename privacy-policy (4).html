Privacy Policy

FILRUP LLC  ·  Effective Date: May 29, 2025  ·  Last Updated: May 29, 2026

FILRUP LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the FILRUP mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. Please read this policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Voice Data: When you use voice-powered features, audio is processed to recognize fuel-related commands. Audio is not stored after processing.
  • Fuel & Vehicle Data: Fuel fill-up records, mileage, fuel type, and vehicle information you enter or speak.
  • Usage Data: App interaction data, feature usage frequency, and crash/diagnostic reports.
  • Device Data: Device type, operating system version, and app version.
  • Location Data: Approximate location, only if you grant permission, to identify nearby fuel stations.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the App's core fuel-tracking features
  • To process voice commands and return accurate responses
  • To display fuel history, spending summaries, and analytics
  • To support bilingual (English/Spanish) functionality
  • To send important service-related notifications
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve performance

3. Data Storage and Security

Your fuel data is stored securely on your device and, if you enable cloud sync, on encrypted servers. We use industry-standard safeguards including TLS encryption in transit and AES-256 encryption at rest. No security system is impenetrable; we encourage you to use a strong passcode on your device.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist with app hosting, analytics, or crash reporting, bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party SDKs (e.g., Apple's speech recognition frameworks). These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review Apple's Privacy Policy at apple.com/legal/privacy.

6. Children's Privacy

FILRUP is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access & Correction: You may access and update your data within the App at any time.
  • Deletion: You may delete your account and all associated data from within the App settings.
  • Voice Permissions: You may revoke microphone access at any time via your device settings.
  • Location Permissions: You may revoke location access at any time via your device settings.

8. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete it, and the right to opt out of its sale (we do not sell personal data). To exercise your rights, contact us at the address below.

9. International Users

FILRUP is based in Arizona, USA. If you access the App from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
