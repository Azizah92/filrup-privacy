Voice or manual entry

Log a fill-up hands-free in one breath, or type it in manually. The button turns green the moment FILRUP hears your amount.

GPS station detection

FILRUP detects the gas station you're at — name and address — so you never have to look it up or type it.

Full fill-up details

Capture gallons, price per gallon, odometer reading, and payment method — cash, debit, or credit — on every stop.

Unlimited vehicles

Add as many vehicles as you like and track each one separately — perfect for families, second cars, and work trucks.
